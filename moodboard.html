05 — Design Principles
Guiding rules for every design decision
01
Trust Before Flash
Every element should increase confidence. If it doesn't build trust, it doesn't belong on the page.
02
One CTA Per Section
Clear hierarchy. One action per viewport. No competing links or buttons splitting attention.
03
Data Speaks First
Metrics, percentages, revenue numbers — lead with proof. Let the data do the selling, not the adjectives.
04
Mobile-First, Always
70%+ of PH traffic is mobile. The phone experience is the primary experience. Design for thumbs.
05
Restraint Is Premium
Whitespace is a feature. Every pixel earns its place. Premium comes from discipline, not decoration.
06
System, Not Personality
The system is the authority. Founder-led but not guru-driven. Let the work speak louder than the person.
06 — Brand Avoidance
What the brand must never look or feel like
No Hype Energy
No "10x your revenue" claims. No fire emojis. No bro-marketing tone.
No Stock Photos
No smiling people at laptops. No handshake photos. No fake corporate imagery.
No Visual Noise
No excessive gradients, competing accents, rainbow palettes, or neon overload.
No Generic AI Branding
No "AI-powered growth machine" language. No robot visuals. No futuristic gimmicks.
